## Post 1

Theme:
Consistency beats creativity.

Summary:
Justin argues that successful creators do not rely on inspiration. Instead, they build repeatable systems that allow them to publish consistently over time.

Key Insight:
Content systems outperform occasional bursts of creativity.

Potential Application for B2B SaaS:
